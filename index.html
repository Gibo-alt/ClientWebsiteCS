Behind The Music

Honest songwriting, soft indie-pop textures, and emotion at the center of every track.

Colin Scott's music blends intimate lyrics with a stripped-back bedroom-pop feel, creating songs that sound personal, reflective, and real. His writing leans into love, vulnerability, growth, and the quiet moments people carry with them, giving each release a sense of closeness that feels more like a conversation than a performance.

About Colin Scott

Colin Scott is a very small singer-songwriter from South Africa, producing music in a bedroom-style setup. As an early-stage independent artist in his early 20s, he's building his audience organically through platforms like Spotify, YouTube, and social media.

His music features emotional, personal lyrics with a soft pop/indie vibe and simple production that captures the raw essence of new artists. With around 600+ monthly listeners on Spotify, Colin is steadily growing his presence through TikTok, Reels, and the Spotify algorithm.

He's passionate about creating authentic music that resonates with listeners, writing both original songs and covers that tell stories of life, love, and personal growth.
